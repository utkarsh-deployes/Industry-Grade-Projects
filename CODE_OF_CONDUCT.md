# Code of Conduct

This repository is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment for everyone who participates.

We value thoughtful discussion, constructive feedback, and collaboration grounded in mutual respect.

---

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:
- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Offer constructive and relevant feedback
- Disagree professionally without personal attacks
- Be open to feedback and different perspectives
- Maintain a professional tone in all interactions

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Demeaning, hostile, or inflammatory language
- Dismissive or condescending responses
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe or unwelcoming environment

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all repository spaces, including:
- Issues
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- Comments and reviews

It applies to all contributors, maintainers, and participants.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Unacceptable behavior may result in:
- A warning
- Content removal
- Temporary or permanent restriction from participation

Actions will be taken with fairness and discretion.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or observe behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the repository maintainers by opening a private issue or contacting them directly.

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.

---

## Final Note

This repository aims to maintain a professional and welcoming space focused on learning, growth, and quality.

Respectful collaboration is essential to achieving that goal.
